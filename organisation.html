Our Organisation

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most preeminent public universities.
Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound.
whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting
the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000students annually,
with approximately 2.64% being computer science students.
We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us.
